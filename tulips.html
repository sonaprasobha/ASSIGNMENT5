Fascinating Facts About Tulips

By Flora Fields

Tulips are not just beautiful spring flowers — they also have a rich cultural and economic history. From royal gardens to floral festivals, tulips continue to captivate people worldwide.

Origins and History

Tulips originated in Central Asia and became highly popular in the Ottoman Empire. In the 17th century, the Netherlands experienced "Tulip Mania" — a period when tulip bulbs were traded like currency.

Colorful Meanings

Different colors of tulips symbolize different things: red for love, yellow for cheerfulness, white for forgiveness, and purple for royalty. There are over 3,000 varieties!
